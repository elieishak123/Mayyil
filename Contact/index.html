Car Rental

At Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport and throughout the capital, you'll find a variety of car rental companies ready to serve you. They provide a broad selection of modern, high-quality vehicles tailored to meet diverse needs. In Lebanon, rental cars are easily recognizable by their green license plates.

Taxi & Onsite Transport

Regular taxis are also available at the airport and throughout the city, but they are not metered and their fares are subject to bargain. In addition, there are plenty of private taxi companies you can book with in advance, most of which accept Whatsapp calls or messages. Note that all taxis and buses, whether public or private, have red number plates.
