Shared Taxi (Servees)

As you walk around, you may hear drivers honking at you. Often, it's their way of offering a ride. If you're ready to hop in, just wave them down and ask, “Servees?” This shared taxi service allows drivers to pick up and drop off passengers along their route.

offers two options: single and double. A single Servees typically covers short trips, like traveling from Gemmayzeh to Hamra (around 4 km). Previously, this fare was around 100,000 LBP per person, with longer trips costing a double fare of about 200,000 LBP. However, prices have risen recently, so it's best to confirm the rate before starting your ride, as Lebanese taxis do not use meters.

While navigating the Servees system may take some getting used to, it quickly becomes one of the most affordable and practical ways to travel around.
