Beirut

Beirut, Lebanon's capital, is a historic city that blends ancient culture with modern vibrancy. With a rich heritage spanning over 5,000 years, it boasts diverse architecture, a lively arts scene, and renowned cuisine

Mount Lebanon

Mount Lebanon (Jabal Lubnan) is a beautiful mountain range in Lebanon, famous for its stunning views, green valleys, and ancient cedar trees. It offers outdoor activities like hiking and skiing, making it a popular destination for nature lovers.

North Lebanon

North Lebanon is a scenic region known for its mountains, valleys and Mediterranean coastline. It features charming villages, historic sites, and delicious Lebanese cuisine. Key attractions include Tripoli, with its historic architecture, and the beautiful Qadisha Valley

South Lebanon

South Lebanon is a beautiful region known for its hills, valleys, and Mediterranean coastline. It features historic sites like Tyre, famous for its Roman ruins, and charming villages. The area offers delicious local cuisine and outdoor activities, making it a great destination for nature and culture enthusiasts.

Beqaa Valley

The Beqaa Valley is a fertile region in Lebanon, nestled between two mountain ranges. Known for its rich agriculture, it produces grapes, apples, and vegetables. The valley also features historical sites, including Roman ruins, and has a diverse cultural landscape. Its climate is hot in summer and mild in winter.

Akkar

Akkar, in northern Lebanon, is a picturesque tourist destination known for its beautiful mountains and valleys ideal for hiking. Visitors can explore historic sites like the Crusader Castle of Qalaat al-Numan and experience local culture in traditional villages.

Baalbek

Baalbek is a historic city in Lebanon famous for its impressive Roman ruins, including the Temple of Bacchus and the Temple of Jupiter. It attracts visitors with its ancient architecture and rich history. Baalbek also features vibrant local culture, festivals, and traditional markets.

Jbeil

The Lebanese Coast, especially in Jbeil (Byblos), is known for its beautiful beaches and historic sites. Jbeil features ancient ruins and a Roman amphitheater. The area offers lovely beaches for sunbathing and water sports, along with a charming souk filled with shops and restaurants.
