Byblos Castle

Built by the Crusaders in the 12th century, Byblos Castle is one of Lebanon's most iconic castles, offering panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the surrounding city. Within its walls, you'll find a small museum displaying artifacts and exhibits about Byblos' rich history.

Location: Byblos Archaeological Site, near the city center

+

Old Souks of Byblos

The old souks are perfect for finding unique souvenirs, handmade jewelry, pottery, and local crafts. The cobbled streets and rustic stone buildings make it a picturesque place for shopping and photography.

Location: Near Byblos Castle and the city center

Reviews
+

Byblos Public Beach

Byblos Beach is a great spot to enjoy the Mediterranean Sea, go swimming, or relax on the sand. The nearby beach restaurants serve fresh seafood and Lebanese mezze, providing a perfect beach day experience.

Location: Near Byblos Fishing Port

Reviews
+

Boat Ride from Byblos Port

Byblos Port, one of the oldest ports in the world, is now a vibrant spot where you can take a traditional boat ride along the coast, enjoying views of the sea, the historical cityscape, and even the castle from the water.

Location: Byblos Fishing Port

Reviews
+

Seaside Restaurant

Byblos is famous for its seafood restaurants, many of which are located near the port with beautiful sea views. Try local dishes such as grilled fish, octopus, or seafood mezze while enjoying the sound of waves.

Location: Byblos Port area

Reviews
+

Fossil Museum(Memory Of Time)

This small but fascinating museum displays fossilized fish, plants, and other marine life from the nearby area, preserved in limestone.The fossils are millions of years old, and the museum provides an intriguing glimpse into Lebanon's ancient natural history.

Location: Byblos Old Souk area
