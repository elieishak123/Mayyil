LEBANON

Lebanon is a beautiful country with a mix of history, culture, and nature. Visitors can explore ancient ruins, enjoy the lively streets of Beirut, or relax on its Mediterranean beaches. The mountains are perfect for hiking and offer great views. Lebanese food is delicious, and the people are friendly and welcoming. Even though it's small, Lebanon has a lot to offer, making it a great place to visit.
