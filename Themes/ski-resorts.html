Discover Skiing in Lebanon

Snow-capped mountains, vibrant resorts, and thrilling slopes await you in Lebanon.

Skiing in Lebanon: Where Winter Dreams Come True! Enjoy the excitement of skiing in Lebanon’s stunning mountains. With well-equipped resorts, scenic views, and slopes for all skill levels, Lebanon is a hidden gem for winter sports lovers.

Faraya Annual Snow Festival

Experience the magic of winter with Faraya’s Annual Snow Festival in February! Enjoy ski and snowboard competitions, breathtaking firework displays, and cozy bonfires, creating a festive winter wonderland in the mountains.

Mzaar Winter Carnival

Get ready for the lively Mzaar Winter Carnival! Featuring torchlight parades, exciting costume contests, and slope-side games, this festival is a winter favorite. End the night with a snowman-building contest and live music under the stars!

Cedars Snow Sculpture Festival

Celebrate the winter season at the Cedars Snow Sculpture Festival, where artists craft beautiful snow sculptures against the stunning cedar trees. Vote for your favorite sculpture, enjoy live music, and explore local art in this unique winter experience.
