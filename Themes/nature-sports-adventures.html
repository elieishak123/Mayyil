Discover Lebanon's Outdoor Adventures

Camping

Lebanon's camping scene is thriving, offering spectacular options for nature lovers seeking both adventure and tranquility. Campers can pitch their tents overlooking the stunning Mediterranean coast north of Jbail (Byblos), where they can stroll down to the sea for a refreshing swim or explore vibrant underwater life while snorkeling. For a different experience, the enchanting Adonis Valley, steeped in Greek mythology, provides a serene, mystical backdrop for camping amidst lush, green landscapes. A wide range of campsites and eco-lodges cater to families and groups alike, featuring platform tents or cozy bungalows, delicious locally-prepared meals, and well-organized activities. Enjoy guided hikes through picturesque trails, exciting mountain biking adventures, and engaging team-building games. Whether you’re a seasoned camper or a first-timer, Lebanon’s diverse camping options promise unforgettable experiences under the stars! Rafting Adventures

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting on Lebanon's exhilarating rivers, where adventure meets breathtaking scenery! The renowned Nahr al-Litani, Lebanon's longest river, offers rapids that cater to both beginners and seasoned rafters. The vibrant waters of the Nahr al-Joz in the picturesque Qadisha Valley provide an unforgettable journey through lush landscapes and ancient monasteries. For a more adrenaline-fueled experience, tackle the challenging rapids of the Nahr Ibrahim, famous for its stunning gorges and exhilarating twists. With professional guides and well-equipped outfitters ready to assist, you’ll glide through pristine nature while soaking in the beauty of Lebanon's rugged terrain. Paragliding

Soar high above Lebanon’s awe-inspiring landscapes on an unforgettable paragliding adventure! Feel the rush as you glide through open skies, with the Mediterranean coastline and towering mountains stretching out beneath you. Launch from the scenic Qalamoun mountains or the breathtaking cliffs of the Cedars, where you’ll be greeted by panoramic views of lush green valleys, ancient ruins, and sparkling turquoise seas. Drift over Lebanon’s remarkable scenery, spotting historic landmarks, terraced vineyards, and expansive olive groves cascading down to hidden beaches. Whether you’re an experienced flyer or trying paragliding for the first time, expert instructors guide you at every step, ensuring both safety and the best possible views of Lebanon’s unparalleled beauty. Paragliding here isn’t just a thrilling adventure; it’s a journey that lets you connect with Lebanon’s natural and cultural treasures, creating memories that capture the country’s breathtaking spirit and stunning heritage from a one-of-a-kind perspective.

Scuba Diving

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean and discover a hidden underwater paradise in Lebanon! With its stunning marine biodiversity, Lebanon offers some of the best scuba diving experiences in the region. Explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful fish, ancient shipwrecks, and fascinating underwater caves that showcase the rich history of this coastal gem. Dive sites like the renowned Raouche Rocks in Beirut and the captivating underwater landscape of Tyre provide both beginners and experienced divers with unforgettable adventures.

Rock Climbing

Challenge yourself with rock climbing at Lebanon's stunning natural cliffs, where adventure meets breathtaking scenery! Nestled in the majestic Lebanese mountains, you'll find some of the region's most exhilarating climbing spots, offering routes for all skill levels. The towering cliffs of the Qadisha Valley provide not only challenging ascents but also panoramic views of lush valleys and ancient monasteries. For those seeking an adrenaline rush, the cliffs of Tannourine and the rugged terrain of Jeita Grotto offer thrilling challenges amid stunning landscapes.
